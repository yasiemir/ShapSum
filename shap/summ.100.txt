Elsewhere, snow fell from the northern   Rockies to the upper Great Lakes, while unseasonably mild weather continued in the East.

A tornado slammed into a shopping district, a residential area, and a school Wednesday in   Huntsville, AL, killing at least 14 people, injuring nearly 300 and trapping many others in cars   and homes.
The tornadoes were reported in Alabama, Maryland, Delaware,   Georgia, Indiana, Kentucky, Mississippi, New Jersey, North Carolina, Ohio, and South Carolina,   Major damage was caused in Huntsville, AL, where seventeen people were killed, 463 injured,   and 1,000 left homeless.
